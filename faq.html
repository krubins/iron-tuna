Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Iron Tuna for snake and auction drafts.

Does Iron Tuna work for both snake and auction drafts?

Yes. Pick your format on the home page. Auction gives live max-bid math and budget tracking; snake gives a best-available board and survival odds for your next pick.

Do I need an account?

No. Build your free, custom cheat sheet with no signup. An account just lets you save your league and reopen it on draft day.

Can I use my exact league scoring?

Yes. Set your scoring, roster slots, and league size, and every value recalculates to your settings, including superflex and 2-QB.

How much does it cost?

The cheat sheet is free in both formats. The live Draft Day experience is a one-time $9.99.

Can I use it during the live draft?

Yes. It is built to run live and keeps working even if your Wi-Fi drops mid-draft.

What is Value Coach and why is it so awesome!

It advises you on every pick, reading your exact draft against your roster and your league to call the move that wins you the most points: who to target, what they are worth, and when to pivot. You can also ask it anything mid-draft.
