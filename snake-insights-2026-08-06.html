1Cleveland’s team environment should improve more than the public thinks

The Browns hired Todd Monken and Travis Switzer after Baltimore produced a 60% run-action rate, top-four multi-RB usage, first-ranked explosive pass rate and air yards per attempt, and Cleveland also overhauled its offensive line.

The play on the board: Move the player or group up roughly one round, or to the front of the current tier. Target Browns as a secondary breakout offense, especially cheaper pieces.

Projected effect: +6% to +9% offense-level efficiency versus 2025  ·  Confidence: Medium

2New York Giants volume is likely to shift from three-WR spread to heavier TE/RB personnel

League scheme analysis expects less 11 personnel under John Harbaugh and Matt Nagy, with Isaiah Likely and Patrick Ricard signaling two-TE and two-RB sets.

The play on the board: Move the player or group up roughly one round, or to the front of the current tier. Upweight Isaiah Likely and backs, slightly downweight secondary Giants WRs.

Projected effect: WR target concentration drops slightly; TE and RB route value rises  ·  Confidence: High

3Allen’s path to another QB1 finish now runs more through efficiency than raw rushing expansion

He posted six-year lows in passing yards per game and passing TDs in 2025, yet still finished QB1.

The play on the board: Treat as tier-dependent and avoid forcing the pick. Rank him first, but do not expect a major rushing spike.

Projected effect: his median is intact, but ceiling is more +3 passing TDs than +100 rushing yards  ·  Confidence: Medium

4Tennessee’s offense should throw deeper in 2026

Brian Daboll’s recent Giants extremes included both low and then high deep-ball rates, but league analysis specifically expects the Titans’ deep passing to increase after a bottom-10 2025 showing with Cam Ward.

The play on the board: Move the player or group up roughly one round, or to the front of the current tier. Favor Titans WR spike-week bets over “safe floor” builds.

Projected effect: +0.8 to +1.5 aDOT-related boom weeks for Titans WRs, with added volatility  ·  Confidence: Medium
