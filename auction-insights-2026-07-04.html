Auction Insights: July 4, 2026

Five research calls for July 4, 2026, each with the read for your auction room.

Drafting a different format? Read these five for snake draft insights or best ball insights.
OVERPRICEDQBConfidence: Medium

Patrick Mahomes is a reputation trap if drafted as a top-tier fantasy QB

He is rehabbing the torn ACL he suffered in 2025 with no guaranteed Week 1 return, he averaged 21.2 FPPG in only 14 games, new KC rushing dynamics and the Walker/Fields additions likely lower pure pass volume.

Projected effect: -4% to -7% versus an elite-QB price

The auction play: Require a discount; stop bidding once the player or group reaches normal market value. only buy if he falls into the Herbert tier.
OVERPRICEDMarketConfidence: High

Miami is the most fragile fantasy ecosystem in football

The Dolphins signed Malik Willis, extended De’Von Achane, lost Tyreek Hill and Jaylen Waddle, and project with one of the league’s weakest offensive lines. They also face a rough schedule with difficult late cold-weather road games.

Projected effect: -10% to -15% team passing expectation versus league median

The auction play: Require a discount; stop bidding once the player or group reaches normal market value. treat Miami outside Achane as a mostly avoid situation in redraft.
OVERPRICEDRBConfidence: High

Bijan Robinson is still a smash talent, but his market no longer leaves room for hidden upside

He was RB3 in 2025 and projects second in current consensus.

Projected effect: -1% to +4% versus price

The auction play: Require a discount; stop bidding once the player or group reaches normal market value. excellent pick, not an exploitable inefficiency.
UNDERPRICEDWRConfidence: High

Jaylen Waddle is a major fantasy winner from moving to Denver

The Broncos traded for him, Denver’s line is elite, and Bo Nix is coming off a QB7 finish.

Projected effect: +12% to +18% versus price if the market is still anchored to damaged Miami context

The auction play: Bid a few dollars above baseline value where needed; do not let the room win this at a flat sheet price. strong upside WR2 target.
UNDERPRICEDTEConfidence: High

Trey McBride is one of the few expensive tight ends still likely to return value

He was TE1 in 2025 PPR by a huge margin and projects for 113.6 catches and 1,099.7 yards in 2026.

Projected effect: +5% to +10% versus price because the role is truly elite

The auction play: Bid a few dollars above baseline value where needed; do not let the room win this at a flat sheet price. pay up if you want a weekly structural edge.
