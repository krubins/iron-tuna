Target Josh Downs as Indianapolis opens up

The Colts traded Michael Pittman Jr. to Pittsburgh, vacating roughly 111 targets, and the team has said it wants to expand Josh Downs with more snaps on the outside in 2026. Downs is a clear bet to absorb volume in that reshaped receiver room. Target the ascending slot man at a mid range WR price before the market fully prices in the bump.

Rachaad White settles in with Jayden Daniels

Rachaad White signed a one year deal in Washington and reunites with his Arizona State teammate Jayden Daniels, and he drew praise from the staff coming out of minicamp. In a committee with Jacory Croskey-Merritt, White is the proven passing down piece. Target him as a cheap PPR dart and pay up only for the early down work elsewhere.

George Pickens is off holdout watch

Dallas placed the franchise tag on George Pickens, and he confirmed he will not hold out, reporting to the team facility and minicamp. The availability cloud that scared off some managers is gone. Bid on Pickens with confidence as a WR2 with WR1 upside in a pass heavy Cowboys attack.

Matthew Golden inherits the Doubs role in Green Bay

Romeo Doubs left Green Bay for New England, and the Packers expect second year wideout Matthew Golden to absorb that vacated volume; Doubs led the team in targets last season. Golden is a textbook year two breakout candidate. Buy the ascending receiver before his price catches up to the opportunity.

Pay up for Ashton Jeanty as a workhorse

Reporting out of Las Vegas indicates the Raiders intend to use Ashton Jeanty as a true workhorse in his second season, and he is going in the late first round. Workhorse volume on a back this talented is exactly what wins auctions. Pay up; the projected touch share makes him a smash value if he stays healthy.

Jonathon Brooks is a cheap Carolina dart

Cleared from his ACL recovery, Jonathon Brooks projects as the Panthers RB2 behind Chuba Hubbard now that Rico Dowdle signed in Pittsburgh. Brooks carries real standalone and contingent value at a late round cost. Grab him as a cheap dart who could vault into a lead role if Hubbard misses time.
