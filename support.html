Support

Questions, bugs, or help with your draft? We're here.

Email us: support@irontuna.com. We usually reply within a day. Including your league size, scoring, and a screenshot helps us help you faster.
How do referrals work?

Open Share & Earn in the app to get your personal referral code and link, then send it to friends. They get $1 off the Full Site ($9.99), and you earn $3 for every friend who buys with your code. We send your $3 by PayPal to the email tied to your code once their purchase clears, after the season's drafts wrap up. There's no cap on how many friends you can refer.

How much does it cost, and can I get a refund?

The cheat sheet is free in both snake and auction formats. The live Draft Day experience is a one-time $9.99 for the current season, with no subscription. If something isn't working right, email support@irontuna.com and we'll make it right.

I paid but don't see Draft Day unlocked.

Reopen Iron Tuna in the same browser you bought it in, or sign in with the email you used at checkout. If it still isn't showing, email support@irontuna.com with your receipt and we'll restore it.

Do I need an account?

No. You can build your free, custom cheat sheet with no signup. An account just lets you save your league and reopen it on draft day.

Is my data private?

Your draft state and league settings live in your browser. See our Privacy Policy for the details.
